Harrods is a department store located on Brompton Road in Knightsbridge, London, England. It is home to many of the world's most famous fashion brands. It is one of the world's largest and most famous department stores in Europe. The Harrods motto is Omnia Omnibus Ubique, which is Latin for "all things for all people, everywhere". Several of its departments, including the seasonal Christmas department and the food halls, are well known.
